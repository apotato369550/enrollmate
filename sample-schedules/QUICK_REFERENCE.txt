─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✨ TEST SCENARIOS:

• Morning Schedule: Set Earliest=08:00, Latest=13:00
• Afternoon Schedule: Set Earliest=12:00, Latest=18:00
• Avoid Full Sections: Uncheck "Allow full sections"
• Avoid At-Risk: Uncheck "Allow at-risk sections"
• Compact Schedule: Try to fit all on MW or TTh only
